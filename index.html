THE 2D-CHALLENGE

How it works

You are given a cross section down the middle of an F1 car's chassis. Using pretty much any 2D design software, design the rear/front wings, bodywork and the floor to fit. Once that's designed, you send it to me for testing. I will find the coeffecient of lift and drag, and convert them into performance charachteristics in Ultimate Racing 2D 2. From there I will race the cars on the saturday of each real life Grand Prix weekend, posting the results and video to match alongside.

As another dimension to this game/challenge, drivers will be able to be traded between teams.



The Rules

1.0 General
1.1 The car must be able to form one closed section i.e there are no floating parts. Exceptions to this rule are found in sections 2 and 3.
1.2 There must be no part of the car touching the bottom of the supplied image i.e the car has some ground clearance.
1.3 There must be no part of the car touching the edges of the supplied image.

2.0 Front Wing
2.1 There may be up too 2 floating sections of the front wing.

3.0 Rear Wing
3.1 There may be up too 2 floating sections of the rear wing.
